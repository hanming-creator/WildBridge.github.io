About WildKin & Our Mission

The Nonprofit

WildKin is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to environmental education. Our mission is to use digital technology to reconnect people with the natural world, specifically the unique ecosystems of Southern California.

The App

Our app combines virtual pet mechanics with real-world conservation data. By adopting wild animals and building their habitats, users learn about the biodiversity of the chaparral, coastal bluffs, and mountain ranges.
