More Than Just a Game

WildKin is designed to bridge the gap between digital play and environmental stewardship. Through our interactive experience, we teach users about the real-world wild animals of California, fostering a deep appreciation for the natural environments they call home. Our goal is to inspire the next generation of conservationists.
