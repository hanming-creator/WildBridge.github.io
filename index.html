Your Wild Journey Begins Here.

Adopt wild animals, build natural habitats, and discover the beauty of Southern California's wilderness in an interactive virtual world.

WildKin is a mobile game where players adopt virtual wildlife companions inspired by Southern California species. By caring for animals, restoring habitats, and learning about native wildlife, players gain a greater appreciation for conservation.

Adopt and Protect

Start your journey by adopting a virtual wild companion. From elusive mountain lions to playful birds, each animal has its own story.

Build Your Sanctuary

Use decorations and natural elements to expand your habitats. Inspired by the diverse ecosystems of Southern California, you can visit everything from sun-drenched chaparrals to serene coastal bluffs.

How It Works

Adopt

Adopt a variety of virtual wildlife companions inspired by real Southern California species, and visit them in their habitats.

Care

Feed your wildlife friends and play mini-games to build friendship and earn coins. Level up friendship to earn tokens.

Decorate

Unlock natural decorations for each pet in the shop to build habitats inspired by each animal's ecosystem.

Learn

Discover fun facts and complete your Conservation Journal as you adopt new species and visit their biomes.

Collect

Increase friendship, unlock achievements, and build your collection while learning about the wildlife that calls Southern California home.

More Than Just a Game

Founded by a high school student with a passion for biology and wildlife, our nonprofit is dedicated to inspiring curiosity about nature through interactive education. By introducing people to the unique animals and habitats of Southern California, we hope to foster a deeper appreciation for local wildlife and encourage the protection of the ecosystems we all share.
