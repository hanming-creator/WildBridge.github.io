Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19th, 2026

Thank you for using WildKin, developed by WildBridge. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your information is collected and used.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your encrypted account credentials (if applicable)

If you use the app while logged in, we may also store:

  • Game progress
  • Animals you have adopted
  • Friendship levels
  • Coins and achievements
  • Habitat decorations and unlocked content
  • App settings and preferences

If you choose to make a donation or purchase, payment information is securely processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your credit card or payment information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Sync your progress across your devices (if supported)
  • Improve the app and fix technical issues
  • Respond to questions or support requests
  • Help ensure the security of user accounts

We do not sell your personal information.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share information only when:

  • Required by law
  • Necessary to protect the security of our users or the app
  • Working with trusted service providers that help operate the app (such as secure cloud hosting or authentication services)

These providers are only permitted to use your information to provide their services.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. While no online system can guarantee absolute security, we strive to keep your information secure.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be family-friendly and educational.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is necessary to create and manage an account. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information may contact us to request its deletion.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Update your account information.
  • Delete your account (if account deletion is supported within the app).
  • Contact/Delete account us to request deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable legal requirements.

Deleting your account may permanently remove your saved game progress.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Apple App Store
  • Apple In-App Purchases
  • Cloud storage or authentication providers (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If significant changes are made, we will update the Effective Date above and may provide notice within the app.

Contact/Delete Account

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact/delete account us using the information on the contact/delete account page.
